BSU’s commitment to the SDGs

BSU is actively committed to fostering sustainable development both directly and indirectly by shaping SDG policies and facilitating their implementation. BSU actively collaborates with governmental, international institutions, and non-governmental organizations (NGOs) in organizing events focused on SDGs and their elements. The University department of Gender and Applied Psychology also plays a vital role in international conferences, such as the worldwide feminine international conference in 2020
